SOLUTIONS

We offer online solutions for language teachers, schools, and universities. From technical to marketing help, we assist and provide professional support.

CLASSROOM SOLUTION

Skype is good program for remote communication but it is not suitable as an online classroom. We will provide you the best classroom software with state of the art features. Whiteboard, Scheduling, Recording, Webcam, Cloud Storage, User control, Media player. All can be done with our virtual classroom.

TEACHER SOLUTION

Individual teachers or a small team of teachers who want to get their own language teaching business instead of working for a school. You must be good at teaching, have the ability to create learning products, and have the capacity to work online/remotely.

SCHOOL SOLUTION

We are doing a project to help schools build their online channel or virtual channel. In this project, the students are able to enroll online, join the school's social activities online, visit the school library online, shop school products online, and can do much more. This product is like a virtual campus, and your students will get everything online.

WE ARE PROFESSIONALS

We are a team of professionals dedicated to provide valuable educational services online. We believe language education can be done virtually, and we are here to help language teachers make a living online & remotely.
